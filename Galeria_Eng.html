Soul in the Wood

Explore this selection of the most representative works from "Soul in the Wood", art with driftwood. Gabi presents this virtual exhibition made with passion, talent, and love for nature.
These pieces not only beautify the space but also evoke a deep connection with the environment from which they originate and prompt reflection on their transformation.
They are unique pieces of wood sought and selected by the artist himself on the Costa Brava. Subsequently, Gabi treats and cares for the wood to add his vision and create original and irreplaceable artistic works.
The works displayed are not yet finished; Gabi is constructing supports and giving the final touches to the wood. He has chosen to showcase them to share his art even while in progress.
Immerse yourself in this artistic journey where driftwood becomes a bridge between nature and human expression.
