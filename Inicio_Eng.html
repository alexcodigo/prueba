About the Artist

Gabriel Ramírez is an artist from the Costa Brava who merges his two great passions: the Sea and Art, transforming driftwood from the Mediterranean coast into unique artworks that combine natural beauty with his artistic vision and expression. Signed as gabiart, each piece reflects a deep respect for nature and a talent for highlighting the inherent textures, shapes, and colors of the material.
Gabi walks along the coast daily, engaging in various activities that allow him to fully enjoy it. This creates the opportunity to find unique pieces of driftwood that the sea gifts to the shore. Inspired by these discoveries, Gabi has created this art gallery called "Soul in the Wood".

Works of Art

Wood Art

Explore organic beauty at its finest. Each piece of natural art is created using driftwood from the Costa Brava, transforming it into an original and unique work of art under the gabiart signature.

- creative process -

Search and Selection

Traveling along the Costa Brava, searching for those original pieces of wood with the potential to be transformed into works of art, selecting them carefully and respectfully.

Treatment and Care

The pieces of wood require specific treatment and care, which Gabi applies with effort and delicacy in his art workshop, striving to preserve the pure essence of the wood.

Completed Works

With talent and dedication, the transition is made from a natural piece of wood to an original and unrepeatable work of art. Always different in many ways, such as expressiveness, dimension, or impact.
